Nada Surf are Indifferent to the Mainstream

13 February 2012

Matthew Pate

About a week or two before the album dropped on January 24, 2012, NPR began streaming Nada Surf‘s new album, The Stars Are Indifferent to Astronomy, in its entirety. I listened to the album about six times straight through, two times a day, on my way to and from work.


Without fail, every Nada Surf release reminds me of adolescence and how, regardless of the stress and disappointment, there’s always something to be optimistic about. This reminiscence isn’t due to the lyrics, rather it is due to the sloppy arrangement of instruments which the lyrics combine with. Nada Surf reminds me of a teenage garage band breaking in to the scene.
