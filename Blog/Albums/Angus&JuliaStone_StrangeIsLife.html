Angus & Julia Stone - Life Is Strange

09 October 2021

Matthew Pate

I will preface this review by stating that I have never considered myself an Angus & Julia Stone mega fan. I have; however, always enjoyed when their tracks would grace my speakers. Big Jet Plane, Heart Beats Slow, Chateau, and Santa Monica Dream are all great tracks, yet the entire Life Is Strange album pales in comparison. The soft vocals persist, yet I find the lyrics lacking any genuine emotion.

Everything gained in the opening verse of Love Song is quickly lost. The moments where hints of The Beta Band peak through, are hastily snuffed. The foundational elements of a good record seem to be present and non-existent at the same time. The album could easily be stripped down to a single or EP without losing much, if anything at all.

Then I remember that this album was written as a soundtrack to a game. So let's call it a soundtrack, not Angus & Julia Stone's fifth studio album. Check out the single, Love Song from Life Is Strange below. If only for the first verse, We can leave this place / We can leave this town and / Never look down....

Then move on to the next album you've been putting off listening.
