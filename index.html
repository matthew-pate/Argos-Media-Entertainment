Still Woozy - If This Isn't Nice...

29 August 2021

Matthew Pate

Having had the chance to see Still Woozy at a music festival and sample some of his tracks in 2019, I was moderately intrigued and looking forward to his debut album, If This Isn’t Nice, I Don’t Know What Is.

Setting the stage with Woof, Sven Eric Gamsky (Still Woozy), treats the listener with a tempo and beat reminiscent of Ra Ra Riot and Hot Chip while, offering a peak into his mind as he sings,

There you go, your ducks in a row / So many things you'll never know / If you want it I can show you but....You wouldn't last a day in my head....You'd probably go insane / You's probably never be the same again....
